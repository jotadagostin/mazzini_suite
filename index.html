About Me

My name is Paolo and I am the owner of this beautiful suite located in the historic center of Verona. Mazzini suite is a 50m2 suite located in a 14th century building which was completely renovated in 2005. You can experience the magic of Verona, just a few meters from the Arena overlooking Piazza Bra' and Via Mazzini, strolling on the "liston", enjoying aperitifs or lunches and dinners in the restaurants overlooking the Arena. You are also within walking distance of all the other marvels that beautiful Verona offers its tourists: the home of Giulietta, Piazza delle Erbe, Piazza dei Signori, which the Veronese call Piazza Dante, because it is in the centre find his monument, the Scaliger Arches, many churches and museums and last but not least Castelvecchio e Castel San Pietro. I will be at your disposal to give you all the necessary information to be able to spend your stay in the most beautiful way, also discovering the city not only through the eyes of the tourist.
